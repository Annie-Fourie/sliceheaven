Our Story

Slice Heaven was born from a passion for authentic Italian pizza and a desire to bring that experience to our community. Our founder, Giovanni Rossi, grew up in Naples learning the art of pizza-making from his grandfather.

After years of perfecting his craft in Italy, Giovanni brought his family recipes and traditional techniques to create Slice Heaven. We still follow the same time-honored methods today, from hand-kneading our dough daily to using only the finest imported ingredients.

What sets us apart is our commitment to quality and authenticity. Our wood-fired oven reaches 900°F, cooking pizzas in just 90 seconds for that perfect Neapolitan-style crust - crispy on the outside, soft and airy on the inside.
